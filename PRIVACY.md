# Privacy Policy for Netflix ScreenShare Fix

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## Data Collection

**Netflix ScreenShare Fix does NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

## What the Extension Does

This extension:
- Applies CSS styling to Netflix video elements in your browser
- Stores your enable/disable preference locally in your browser's localStorage
- Does not access, collect, or transmit any personal information
- Does not track your browsing history
- Does not access your Netflix account information
- Does not communicate with any external servers

## Permissions Explained

### scripting
Used to inject CSS styling into Netflix pages to fix the black screen issue during screen sharing.

### activeTab
Allows the extension to interact with the current Netflix tab when you click the extension icon.

### declarativeContent
Enables the extension to show its icon only when you're on Netflix.com.

### host_permissions (*.netflix.com)
Required to access Netflix pages and apply the CSS fix. Limited strictly to Netflix domains only.

## Local Storage

The extension uses browser localStorage to remember your on/off preference. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to any external parties
- Can be cleared by disabling or removing the extension

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.
